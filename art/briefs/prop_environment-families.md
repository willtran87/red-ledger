# Environment Prop Families

## Shared Direction

- Early-1990s 2.5D first-person game sprite source.
- Catastrophe-modernist corporate horror with chunky, practical construction.
- Hard grouped upper-front-left lighting and a shared invisible ground baseline.
- Paper white, toner black, charcoal steel, signal red, safety yellow, cool blue-gray, and aged brass.
- Exactly six equal cells per source sheet, ordered left to right, with no overlap or cell-boundary spill.
- Perfectly flat `#00FF00` chroma background; green is forbidden in the subjects.
- No real company names, wordmarks, slogans, logos, recognizable buildings, or branded uniforms.
- No text, labels, dividers, floor plane, cast shadow, contact shadow, reflection, or translucent material.

## Runtime Treatment

Fixed-column extraction is mandatory. Each base is fitted once to its locked canvas and pivot, then quantized without dithering. Damaged and powered states are deterministic derivatives of the base frame so outline, mass, perspective, and equipment placement cannot drift.
