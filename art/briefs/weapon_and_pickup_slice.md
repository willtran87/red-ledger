# Vertical Slice Weapons, Pickups, and Adjuster Brief

## Shared direction

- Original early-1990s 2.5D first-person game art with catastrophe-modernist corporate horror.
- Paper white, toner black, charcoal steel, signal red accents; safety yellow and screen cyan only where specified.
- Chunky practical construction, hard grouped upper-front-left light, readable silhouettes after low-resolution reduction.
- Fictional Red Ledger Mutual setting. A mark, when necessary, is three offset roof panels above a square.
- No umbrella forms, company names, wordmarks, slogans, recognizable third-party game art, legible text, watermark, signature, frame, floor, or shadow.
- Every generated cutout uses a uniform #00FF00 chroma background and opaque edges.

## Shared hands

Gender-ambiguous adult catastrophe-adjuster hands in charcoal work gloves with off-white fabric backs and one narrow signal-red seam. Hands are sturdy, practical, and identical across the arsenal.

## Weapons

- Staple Driver: compact pneumatic industrial stapler, squared off-white and steel housing, small red pressure gauge.
- Twin-Bore Riveter: broad two-channel salvage riveter, stacked pressure chambers, mechanical break-open recoil.
- Audit Repeater: narrow motorized document perforator, rotating feed drum, paper-strip ejection port.
- Catastrophe Launcher: heavy suppression-canister projector, open red canister cradle, warning-yellow latch.

Each first-person weapon is centered, mildly asymmetric around its operating hand, aimed at the upper center, with a 60 mm-equivalent perspective. Muzzle flashes are separate and omitted.

## Pickups

All pickups use a front three-quarter product angle and an obvious base. Silhouettes remain distinct without relying on hue. Small pickups use 48x48 runtime canvases; large pickups use 64x64. Runtime shine frames preserve the base silhouette and are produced deterministically from the approved base.

## Adjuster portrait

Gender-ambiguous adult, close-cropped dark hair, charcoal field-jacket collar, pale paper-reflected upper-front-left key light. Five damage tiers accumulate soot, toner, abrasions, torn collar, and exhaustion without changing facial structure, hairline, camera, collar, or lighting. The runtime portrait is 28x28.
